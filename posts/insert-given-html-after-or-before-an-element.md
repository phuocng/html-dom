title: Insert given HTML after or before an element
---

### Insert after

Insert `html` after the `ele` element:

```js
ele.insertAdjacentHTML('afterend', html);
```

### Insert before

Insert `html` before the `ele` element:

```js
ele.insertAdjacentHTML('beforebegin', html);
```
